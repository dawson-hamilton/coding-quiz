Coding Quiz

The instructions are simple! Just press the start button below and in will start the quiz. When you get into the quiz you will be shown a question and you will also be provided with four option for the answer to that question. Your score will be kept and at the end you can see your highscore as well! This quiz will be timed and every time you get a question wrong there will be 15 seconds taken off the timer. If you get the answer right then the timer will just keep ticking as usual. your final score will be how many seconds you had left on the clock.
